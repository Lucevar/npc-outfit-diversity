.More Argonian Hair.

=== Install ===
Extract the folders to your Morrowind/Data folder.


=== Description ===
Adds several argonian hairstyles. They are variations of vanilla assets.
Use _Plus NPCs version if you want to see the hairstyles on (over 60) NPCs and character creation.
Hairstyles considered a bit over-the-top and were not included on NPC heads. :)

8 Feathered
7 Fins
10 Horns
3 Mixed

=== Usage/Permissions ===
It's all variations of vanilla assets. 
They may be freely distributed/edited (for Morrowind purposes).
